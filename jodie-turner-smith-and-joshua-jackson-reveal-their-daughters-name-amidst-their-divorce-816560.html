Jodie Turner-Smith and Joshua Jackson Reveal Their Daughters Name Amidst Their Divorce

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Adding to the series of regrettable divorces this year, British actress and model Jodie Turner-Smith have initiated divorce proceedings from her spouse, actor Joshua Jackson. The couple, who previously garnered attention for their mutual support and affection, is now making headlines for their impending separation.

There are some requests regarding their daughter in the process of their split. Also, their daughter’s name was finally revealed after three years.

The couple’s marital bliss lasted for three years before their divorce.

VALERIE MACON/AFP/East News

Jodie and Joshua’s relationship kicked off at a birthday celebration in 2018 and quickly advanced. In a swift turn of events, Jodie proposed to Joshua, and they clandestinely tied the knot in 2019.

While breakup speculations arose in September 2022 following their mutual unfollowing on Instagram, the couple dispelled these rumors when they made a joint appearance at the Oscars in March.

The couple’s daughter’s name was finally revealed.

Dylan Travis/ABACA/Abaca/East News

Jodie and Joshua are known for keeping their daughter’s life private. However, along with their divorce papers, the name of their now three-year-old daughter has finally been revealed. They named the little girl Juno Rose Diana Jackson, born in April 2020.

Special request regarding their daughter.

Jodie Turner-Smith has started the divorce process with Joshua Jackson. She is stating that they have “irreconcilable differences.”

Unlike many couples who may seek sole custody, Jodie and Joshua have chosen a more considerate and unique approach. Jodie, desiring her daughter to have ample time with her father, is requesting joint custody of their three-year-old, Juno.

No prenuptial agreement had been arranged.

Dylan Travis/ABACA/Abaca/East News

Jodie doesn’t want spousal support from Joshua or for herself, but she’s asking him to cover her lawyer’s fees. They officially separated on September 13, which is strange because Jodie had posted pictures with Joshua on Instagram a few days earlier, and they were seen holding hands just a day before. Joshua hasn’t commented on the divorce yet.
