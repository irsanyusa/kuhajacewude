20+ Photos of Stars Proving That a Mix of Races Always Leads to Incredible Appearances

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Very often, people of mixed nationalities have very attractive appearances. Studies also show that mixed-race people seem more attractive.

We at Bright Side have looked through the biographies of celebrities and found that many stars have ancestors of different races.

1. Sandra Bullock

Sandra Bullock was born in Washington, but before the age of 12, she spent most of her time in Nürnberg. Her father is American and her mother is German.

2. Sylvester Stallone

Sylvester Stallone is of Italian, French, and Eastern European descent.

3. Tilda Swinton

The actress was born in London. She comes from the ancient Scottish family of Swinton, and her mother is Australian. The actress is Scottish.

4. Kate Winslet

Kate Winslet is of British descent but also has Irish ancestry on her father’s side and Swedish ancestry on her mother’s side.

5. Amanda Seyfried

The actress has German, English, Scottish, Irish, and Welsh roots.

6. Adrien Brody

Adrien Brody’s father is of Polish-Jewish descent, and his mother is the daughter of a Hungarian aristocrat and a Czech-Jewish mother.

7. Madonna

Madonna’s father’s parents were Italian, and her mother’s parents were French Canadian.

8. Henry Ian Cusick

Henry Ian Cusick was born in Peru. His mom is Peruvian, and his dad is Scottish. When he was only 2 years old, the family moved to Spain, and later, to Trinidad and Tobago.

9. Renée Zellweger

Renée Zellweger’s father is Swiss, and her mom is of Norwegian and Kven descent.

10. Julia Roberts

The actress’s father has English, Welsh, Scottish, and Irish genes, and her grandmother was Swedish.

11. Matt Damon

The father of the famous actor had English and Scottish roots. His mother was a university professor with Finnish and Swedish roots.

12. Justin Timberlake

Justin Timberlake has English, Irish, Scottish, Welsh, Dutch, French, and German roots, and ancestors from the Jersey Islands.

13. Anne Hathaway

Anne Hathaway was born to a lawyer and an actress. Her mother is Irish and her father has French, German, English, and Irish roots.

14. Jennifer Lawrence

Jennifer Lawrence has English, Irish, Scottish, and German genes.

15. Bella Hadid

The supermodel was born in Washington, D.C to a family of a Dutch model and an American from Palestine.

16. Selena Gomez

Selena Gomez was born in 1992 in Texas. Her father is Mexican and her mother is Italian.

17. Justin Bieber

18. Sarah Jessica Parker

Sarah Jessica Parker’s father is Jewish and from Eastern Europe, and her mother has German and English blood.

19. Robert Downey Jr.

The father of the actor is half-Lithuanian-Jewish, 1/4 Hungarian-Jewish, and 1/4 Irish. His mother has German, Swiss, and Scottish roots.

20. Cher

Cherilyn Sarkisian has an Armenian American father and a mother whose predecessors are Irish, English, German, and Cherokee.

21. Lara Fabian

Lara Fabian is the only daughter born to a Belgian father and a Sicilian mother.

22. Viggo Mortensen

Viggo Mortensen has a mix of different nationalities, including Danish, American, Canadian, and Norwegian.
