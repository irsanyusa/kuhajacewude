How 15 Same-Age Pairs of Celebs Looked on Red Carpets in the 2000s vs Now

June 2024 · 1 minute read

Low-rise or extremely baggy jeans, pointy below-the-knee boots, and even fedora hats — all of these fashion trends take us back to the 2000s in a heartbeat. They were everywhere, including in celebrities’ wardrobes, which might explain how they made their way to so many red carpets. Nowadays, the fashion scene is completely different, so the outfits of today’s 23-year-old actress Zendaya compared to the once-23-year-old singer Alicia Keys at award shows are on opposite poles.

Bright Side created a photo display that allows you to jump back and forth in time in order to see the evolution of red carpet looks of famous people at the same age, now and then.
