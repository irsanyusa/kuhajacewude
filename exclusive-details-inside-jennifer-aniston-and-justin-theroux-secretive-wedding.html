Inside Jennifer Aniston and Justin Theroux secretive wedding!

September 2024 · 2 minute read

According to US weekly, the pair even tricked their friends who arrived thinking the wedding ceremony was an early birthday party for Justin, who will be 44 years old on August 10th.

"It was done completely on the sly. It was their closest friends and very simple and intimate. There were lots of laughs as well as tears and the mood was light", an insider told US weekly.

Jennifer and Justin exchanged their wedding vows before friends, Courteney Cox, Lisa Kudrow, Howard Stern, Sia, Chelsea Handler, Jennifer Meyer, Emily Blunt, John Krasinski, Ellen DeGeneres, Lake Bell, and Scott Campbell among others.

The wedding photographer was famous friend, Terry Richardson, while singer, Sia sang her hit "Chandelier."

After the big wedding, guests feasted on chicken, fish, sausage, fettuccine carbonara with pork and mostaccioli pasta with red sauce, another source gushed to Us weekly, “it was really, really good,” adding that the giant vanilla and strawberry cake, topped with two puppets cake was "delicious!”

The top-secret wedding planned was by Jennifer, who managed to pull it off by swearing those close to her to secrecy and not allowing anyone use of their phones.

While this Justin's first wedding, this will be Jennifer's second wedding, her first to Brad Pitt in July 2000 cost a whopping $1 million.

The newlyweds are now on their honeymoon in Bora Bora which they left for on Thursday, August 6th, aboard a private jet, along with Chelsea Handler and other close friends.
